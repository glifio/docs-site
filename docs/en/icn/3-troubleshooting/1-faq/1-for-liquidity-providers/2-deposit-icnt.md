# Deposit ICNT

## What is stICNT?

stICNT is an ERC20 token deployed on ICNT that Liquidity Providers (LPs) hold to earn rewards. As long as Storage Providers (SPs) are borrowing ICNT from the pool, the value of stICNT increases every block, which translates into rewards for LPs. To learn more, click here.

## How can I earn rewards from holding stICNT?

stICNT can be thought of as a 'redemption ticket' for ICNT deposited into GLIF. It allows the stICNT holder to withdraw ICNT from the pool. The amount of ICNT that can be redeemed with 1 stICNT token increases as the pool earns fees from SPs. To learn more, click here.

## How do I provide liquidity in GLIF? / How do I get stICNT?

Liquidity can be provided by depositing ICNT into GLIF on the pool page [here](https://www.glif.io/zh/pool/infinity). In exchange for your deposit, you'll receive a proportionate amount of stICNT tokens at the current stICNT price.

## Where can I see my earnings in GLIF?

You can check the ICNT value of your stICNT holdings by visiting the [wallet](https://www.glif.io/en/wallet) or [multisig](https://www.glif.io/en/multisig) page of a particular address. ICNT earnings will be displayed in the Tokens section:

## Where do my earnings come from in GLIF?

Earnings come from the fees generated by Storage Providers while borrowing ICNT from GLIF. ICNT earnings are split evenly among stICNT holders, and automatically compounded in GLIF.

## How does GLIF profit?

The GLIF Treasury retains 10% of the pool's total earnings. These funds are used to support the development, growth, and security of GLIF.

## How is LP (Liquidity Provider) charged?

There are no charges for LPs.
