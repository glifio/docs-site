# How to mint your GLIF+ LP Card in a multisig wallet

This tutorial explains how to mint and activate a GLIF+ LP Card by depositing iFIL and GLF. Minting a Card allows you to participate in the [GLIF+ program](/docs/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/liquidity-provider-loyalty-card) and receive in-protocol benefits by staking a mix of $GLF and iFIL. If you would like to learn more about the benefits of minting a GLIF+ Card, you can check [this page](/docs/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/benefits-for-liquidity-providers).

If you are using a single-signature wallet, check [this tutorial](/docs/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/glif-lp-card-tutorials/how-to-mint-your-glif-lp-card) for a step by step guide.

***

## Step 1: Start the minting process

1. Go to the **Loyalty** page, or open your **Wallet** page and click **'Mint Today'**.
2. Enter the Card address. If you start from the Wallet page, the address may already be prefilled.

***

## Step 2: Enter iFIL and GLF amounts

1. Enter the amount of **iFIL** and **GLF** you want to add to the Card. This amount does not include the mint cost of **2000 GLF**. To activate the Card, a minimum GLF to iFIL token ratio of 1:1 is required.
2. You may also choose to activate the Card later, in which case you only need to pay the 2000 GLF mint cost.
3. The **'Due now'** amount on the left shows how much iFIL or GLF will be deducted from your wallet in this transaction. The **'iFIL or GLF balance remaining'** on the right shows how much will be left in your wallet after the transaction is completed.
4. Click **'Mint and Activate'** to begin.

> [!NOTE]
> Make sure you have enough GLF to cover both the mint cost and the deposit. The required balances must be held by the multisig wallet, not by individual signer wallets.

***

## Step 3: Approve token transfers

1. Check the details of the card to be minted, including the cost.
2. Click **'Approve iFIL transfer'**. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
3. Click **'Approve GLF transfer'**. Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Wait 1 to 2 minutes for the transaction to be done.

***

## Step 4: Mint the Card

1. Click **'Mint'**.
2. Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Wait 1 to 2 minutes for the transaction to process.

***

## Step 5: Approve multisig proposals

Three proposals will be created automatically:

* two **'Approve'** proposals
* one **'Mint'** proposal

> [!NOTE]
> The **'Mint'** proposal cannot be approved until both **'Approve'** proposals are approved.

1. Navigate to the **Proposals** tab and approve the proposals in the correct order.
2. Confirm each approval in the signer's wallet. Wait 1 to 2 minutes after all approvals are completed.

***

## Step 6: View your minted Card

1. Return to the **Loyalty** tab or your **Wallet** page.
2. Your GLIF+ LP Card should now appear, showing it has been successfully minted and activated.

***

## Conclusion

Your GLIF+ LP Card has been successfully minted and activated. You can now manage your iFIL and GLF balances, execute FIL Capsules, and access GLIF+ LP benefits directly through your Card.

You may want to check out the other [tutorials](/docs/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/glif-lp-card-tutorials-multisig-wallet) to learn more about managing your GLIF+ LP Card.
