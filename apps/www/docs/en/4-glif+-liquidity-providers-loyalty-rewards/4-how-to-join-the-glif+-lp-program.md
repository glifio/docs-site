# How to Join the GLIF+ LP Program

Follow the steps below to start receiving FIL rewards through your GLIF+ LP Card.

## 1. Mint a Card

Liquidity Providers can mint a GLIF+ LP Card directly on the GLIF website [here](https://www.glif.io/en/loyalty). A step-by-step tutorial will be available here (coming soon).

* **Mint cost**: 2,000 $GLF
* Once minted, the Card becomes **soulbound** to your wallet and is **non-transferable**.

***

## 2. Activate Your Card

### Staking Requirement

To activate a GLIF+ LP Card and receive Capsules, the following staking conditions must be met:

* A minimum of **1 iFIL** staked.
* A minimum of **1 $GLF per iFIL** staked.

There are no penalties or fees for adding additional tokens at any time.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Your GLF : iFIL ratio must be **≥ 1:1** to keep the Card active.

### How iFIL and $GLF Affect Rewards

The amount and value of FIL inside each Capsule are determined by two factors:

**1. More iFIL Staked → More FIL appears in each Capsule**

This FIL is distributed pro rata based on iFIL staked across all active LP Cards. More iFIL staked results in a larger share of the FIL allocated during the week.

**2. More $GLF Staked → Lower cost to unlock FIL Capsules**

The amount of $GLF tokens determines the amount of $GLF required to unlock the FIL in your Capsule, which also means the **premium** over market price when redeeming for FIL

***

## 3. Receive FIL Capsules

* Once your Card is active, FIL Capsules will be issued to your wallet automatically. FIL inside each Capsule can be unlocked using $GLF tokens from your staked balance. As long as your Card remains active, Capsules will continue to be issued on schedule. Read more about how FIL Capsules work [here](/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/how-fil-capsules-work).
  * **Frequency**: Every week
  * **Validity Duration**: Each Capsule is valid for 30 days
