# How to refer others using your GLIF+ LP card

This tutorial explains how to use your personal referral link to earn Referral Capsules, each encapsulating 5 FIL. Referral Capsules function the same way as standard FIL Capsules and can be executed using the same process.

For details on how to execute Referral Capsules, click [here](/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/glif-lp-card-tutorials/how-to-execute-your-fil-capsules).

To learn more about how Referral Capsules work, click [this page](/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/glif-lp-referral-program).

***

1. Go to your **'Wallet'** tab. You will see the banner **'Refer a friend, get a free Capsule'**.

![Refer a friend, get a free Capsule banner with share invite link](refer-lp-image-1.png)

2. Click **'Share Invite Link'** or copy the link directly. Share it to your social platforms and friends.

3. Whenever someone uses your link to mint a **GLIF+ LP Card**, you will receive a **Referral Capsule** containing **5 FIL**.

***

## Conclusion

Your referral link is ready to use. By sharing it, you can earn additional FIL through Referral Capsules and continue expanding your FIL redeemable rewards stream.

You may want to check out the other [tutorials](/glif-liquidity-providers-loyalty-rewards/glif-lp-card-tutorials) to learn more about managing your GLIF+ LP Card.
