
# Benefits for token holders

## Earn Rewards by Depositing Filecoin

Filecoin inflates over time, meaning FIL token holders are constantly being diluted by the new FIL tokens entering the Filecoin economy. GLIF allows FIL token holders to protect themselves against network inflation.

## More Liquidity

GLIF has the largest amount of Filecoin in its pool, which allows Liquidity Providers to deposit and withdraw at any time without fees or lockup periods.

## Secure Pool Design

GLIF's top priority is the safety of Liquidity Provider and Storage Provider assets. GLIF has been audited several times, and uses conservative and algorithmic economic policies to minimize risk of loss.
