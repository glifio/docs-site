# Liquidity Provider Loyalty Card

![Liquidity Provider Loyalty Card](../.gitbook/assets/image (272).png)

GLIF+ Liquidity Provider Loyalty Cards allow iFIL and $GLF token holders to receive real, on-chain benefits. GLIF+ is powered by the GLIF+ Loyalty Card, which is a non-transferable NFT that enables users to receive in-protocol benefits by staking a mix of $GLF and iFIL.

Liquidity Providers (LPs) can turn iFIL positions and $GLF tokens into a stream of FIL-redeemable rewards. This mechanism is powered by [FIL Capsules](how-fil-capsules-work.md)—on-chain NFTs that encapsulate a predefined amount of FIL, allowing holders to swap $GLF for FIL at fixed, predetermined rates. Meanwhile, GLIF uses a portion of its Treasury fees to purchase $GLF directly from Card holders at a premium.

Mint your card, stake your tokens, and earn from FIL Capsules. Join the program and put your $GLF and iFIL to work today [here](https://www.glif.io/loyalty).
