# Storage Provider Loyalty Card

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (8).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

GLIF+ Loyalty Cards allow $GLF Token Holders to receive benefits from using GLIF. For Storage Providers, holding a GLIF+ Loyalty Card means increased borrowing limits, and cash back on interest payments. Mint your card, activate a tier, and fund it to increase your borrowing limits and start redeeming cash back on every interest payment. If you’re a Filecoin Storage Provider, join the program and put your $GLF to work today!
